  • KOREAN FLAG? TAEGEUKGI!

    Korean flag is TAEGEUKGI.
    The white background symbolizes purity and peacefulness. The four trigrams symbolize heaven, earth, water, and fire.
    The yin-yang in the middle symbolizes the harmony of the world. Therefore, TAEGEUKGI represents the balance of the universe.
